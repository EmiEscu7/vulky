How to Delete Your Account

This is the guide on how to delete your account from our platform. Follow the steps below:

  1. Step 1: Go to your account settings.
  2. Step 2: Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section.
  3. Step 3: Click the "Delete Account" button and confirm.
  4. Step 4: You will receive a confirmation email.

If you have any issues, please contact support at support@example.com.
